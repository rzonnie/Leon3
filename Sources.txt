Adding Peripherals to the AMBA AHB or APB Bus
The AHB bus is the primary bus with high bandwidth and a complex protocol.
The APB bus is the secondary bus encapsulated in a single device connected to the AHB Bus. It has a lower bandwidth and speed, but is simpler.
